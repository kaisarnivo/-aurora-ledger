1 · Executive Summary

Aurora Ledger is a Cosmos-SDK Layer 1 engineered by NivoNexus for regulated RWAs. Compliance logic, custody attestations, PQ security, and automation hooks live at the base layer, letting issuers tokenize funds, metals, credit, treasuries, and real-estate SPVs using a single gas/governance token (`AUR`).

  • Compliance-by-design with deterministic investor eligibility, Travel Rule capture, and freeze/pause tools.
  • Custody assurance through oracle-verified proof-of-reserves before any supply change.
  • PQC roadmap (Dilithium/Falcon + secp256k1, PQ TLS) for multi-decade security.
  • Automation-native OpenClaw agents for bootstrap, monitoring, and incident response.
  • Private subchains inheriting settlement security and compliance guardrails.

2 · Market Landscape & Opportunity

Tokenized assets are forecast to surpass $10T by 2030 (BCG/ADDX). Adoption is slowed by fragmented compliance, opaque custody, missing PQ roadmaps, and operational friction. Aurora replaces point solutions with a unified, open platform aligned with VARA, FATF, and traditional controls.

3 · Vision & Value Proposition

  • Trust: deterministic enforcement reduces examiner friction.
  • Transparency: regulators view custody deltas and policy changes in real time.
  • Automation: agent-driven ops handle onboarding, monitoring, and freezes.
  • Future readiness: PQ security, modular subchains, programmable bridges.

5 · Compliance & Custody Flow

Aurora’s compliance engine is mapped to the Dubai VARA Rulebooks (Market Conduct, AML/CFT, Technology & Governance).

graph LR
    Investor -->|KYC/KYB| Provider
    Provider -->|Credential Hash| ComplianceCore
    ComplianceCore -->|Eligibility Proof| Transfer
    Custodian -->|Vault Attestation| OracleBridge
    Auditor -->|Audit Hash| OracleBridge
    OracleBridge -->|Quorum Proof| MintBurn
    MintBurn --> Ledger
  1. Approved providers issue signed credentials; hashes + expiry metadata stored in `compliance_core`.
  2. Transfers/mints reference eligibility, jurisdiction, lockups, Travel Rule, and sanctions lists.
  3. Custody quorum (custodian + auditor) signs off before supply changes; automation compares reserves to supply.
  4. Travel Rule automation packages originator/beneficiary hashes for licensed VASPs.

7 · Private Subchains & Automation

Tenant subchains (Cosmos consumer chains / rollups) inherit compliance templates, reference `compliance_core`, and settle to mainnet. Automation agents deploy binaries, configure validators, manage onboarding, and monitor metrics with fail-safes that freeze assets on custody divergence.

8 · Use Cases

  • Institutional Funds: multi-class structures with jurisdiction caps and automated disclosures.
  • Precious Metals: vault attestations (weight, purity, insurance) gate mints; 24/7 proof-of-reserves.
  • Private Credit: borrower KYC + collateral verification, repayment waterfalls on private subchains.
  • Tokenized Treasuries: automated coupon distributions and transparent VARA/FATF reporting.
  • Real-Estate SPVs: property-specific subchains isolate risk while settling on L1.
  • Green Finance: ESG attestations and oracle feeds validating sustainability claims.

12 · Governance & Operations

Phase 0 multisig (NivoNexus + partners) manages upgrades and treasury spend per VARA Technology & Governance requirements. On-chain governance activates post-mainnet (AUR holders).
